How-To Guide

Everything you need to know about using the Collierville Operations Hub

Add to Your Phone (Home Screen App)

You can add the Collierville Operations Hub to your phone's home screen so it opens like a regular app.

iPhone (Safari)
  1. Open the hub URL in Safari (does NOT work in Chrome on iPhone)
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Name it "Collierville Hub" and tap Add
Note: You MUST use Safari on iPhone. "Add to Home Screen" does not appear in other browsers on iOS.
Android (Chrome)
  1. Open the hub URL in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. Name it and tap Add

Using the Checklists

  1. Tap Opening / Closing, Weekly Cleaning, or Prep Sheet from the navigation
  2. Enter your name and tap Start Checklist to begin a session
  3. Check off each task as you complete it — completed items turn green with a strikethrough
  4. Each completed item records your name, the time, and date
  5. Some items have a camera icon — take or upload a photo as proof
  6. The progress bar tracks your completion percentage in real time
  7. When done, tap Submit to Google Sheets to log the data
Tip: Always submit BEFORE resetting so your work is recorded.

Manager Dashboard

  1. Tap Dashboard from the navigation
  2. View live status of all checklists in progress
  3. Review summary cards for tasks done, photos attached, and open items
  4. Check today's snapshot for a quick roll-up of the day
  5. See recent activity history from submitted checklists

Troubleshooting

  • Checkboxes not saving? — Make sure you're not in a private/incognito window.
  • Submit not working? — Check the Google Apps Script URL is configured correctly.
  • Photos not working? — Make sure you allow camera access when prompted.
  • Data from yesterday? — The app auto-resets at midnight each day.
